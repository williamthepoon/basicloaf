A Bit About Me...

So, who's behind Basic Loaf? Just a Dad of two based here in Dublin, juggling a tech job and a serious obsession with baking bread. Like loads of people, I properly got the bug during the lockdowns, went down the sourdough rabbit hole, made some *very* questionable loaves (ask my family!), but eventually figured out a way that actually works without demanding my soul. I started sharing tips with mates, and now I'm putting it all here. Hopefully, it helps you skip the disaster phase and get straight to the good stuff!

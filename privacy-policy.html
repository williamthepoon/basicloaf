Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This privacy policy describes how Basic Loaf ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our website at basicloaf.com.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. However, our website uses third-party services that may collect information automatically, including:

  • Usage data - Pages visited, time spent, and general browsing behaviour, collected via analytics tools
  • Device information - Browser type, operating system, and IP address, collected by hosting and analytics providers
  • Cookies - Small files stored by your browser, used by third-party services we embed (such as Google Fonts and Pinterest)

How We Use Information

Any data collected through our site is used solely to understand how the site is being used and to improve its content. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our site integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google Fonts - Fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP address. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Pinterest - We may use Pinterest features or the Pinterest API to share content. Pinterest may collect data about your interactions. See Pinterest's Privacy Policy.
  • Etsy - Our shop links go to Etsy, which has its own data practices. See Etsy's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Any data held by third-party services is subject to their respective retention policies.

Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. To exercise any of these rights, or if you have any questions about this policy, please get in touch via Instagram: @basicloafsourdough.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top.
